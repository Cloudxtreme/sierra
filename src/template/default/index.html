Our Mission:

I am a fellow traveler who has spent most of his life back packing in the wilderness of North America. The main tenant of backpacking is to leave no trace behind. This means that not only do I leave no impact of my existence, I help reduce the impact of other humans on the environment.

When people travel and leave their house behind in the care of others, it can feel very intrusive. My goal is to give you that peace of mind that when you return home your house and pets will be exactly as you left them without any feeling that someone was watching your house.
